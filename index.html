☀️
Natural light, no flash
Outdoor or near a window. Flash washes out colour grading. Yellow tube light skews yellow tones especially for moong and chana.
📐
Straight overhead, 20–30 cm
Hold phone directly above the sample, not at an angle. This distance fits ~200 grains — enough for accurate sampling.
🌾
Single layer, spread flat
No piling. Spread grain in one layer on a flat surface. Sample from 3 spots in the lot — top, middle, bottom. Traders always show the best grain on top.
🚫
Don't scan through bag or net
Remove from bag first. Net patterns inflate impurity count and distort size grading.
🟡
For processed dal — spread on white plate
Dal halves are flat — they clump together. Spread thin. Check for skin fragments, broken halves, and colour consistency in different parts of the bag.
